# AGNI Smart Fleet AI
## AI-Powered Smart College Transportation Management System

This is not just a Bus Tracking Application.

It is a complete AI-powered Transportation Management Platform that connects Students, Parents, Drivers, Transport Administrators, College Management, and Maintenance Teams into one intelligent ecosystem.

---

## 1. User Roles

The application has six major user roles.

### 1. Student

Students can:
- View their assigned bus
- Track buses in real time
- Receive ETA notifications
- Receive bus arrival alerts
- Request alternative buses
- View bus occupancy
- View route map
- AI travel assistant
- Report issues
- Emergency SOS

### 2. Driver

Drivers can:
- Secure Login
- Start Trip
- End Trip
- Share GPS
- Receive stop reminders
- View waiting students
- Report bus damage
- View route navigation
- Receive AI suggestions
- Emergency SOS

### 3. Parent (Optional)

Parents can:
- Track their child's assigned bus
- Receive pickup alerts
- Receive arrival notifications
- View ETA
- Contact transport office

### 4. Transport Admin

Admin manages:
- Buses
- Drivers
- Routes
- Stops
- Students
- Trips
- Maintenance
- Fuel
- Reports
- AI Analytics

### 5. Maintenance Team

Can:
- View repair requests
- Update repair status
- Upload repair reports
- Schedule maintenance
- Close completed repairs

### 6. Principal / Management

Management dashboard:
- Fleet utilization
- Fuel analytics
- Student statistics
- Bus performance
- Driver performance
- AI reports
- Budget analytics

## 2. Student Module

### Student Login

Secure login via:
- College Email
- Student ID

### Assigned Bus

Every student is permanently assigned to:
- Bus
- Route
- Boarding Stop

Example:
- Student: Rahul
- Assigned Bus: 21A
- Stop: Navalur

Students cannot normally board another bus.

### Live Bus Tracking

Students can see:
- All buses
- Live GPS
- Live movement
- Live speed
- Direction
- Current location

Google Maps updates every few seconds.

### ETA Prediction

Shows:
- Bus: 21A
- Arriving: 4 Minutes

AI calculates ETA using:
- Current speed
- Traffic
- Distance
- Historical travel time

### Bus Arrival Notification

Student receives:
- "Your Bus Arrives in 5 Minutes"
- Then: "Bus Reached Your Stop"

No need to constantly watch the map.

### Stop Reminder

Student selects: My Stop → Navalur

Application remembers forever.

### Seat Availability

Shows:
- Bus Capacity: 52
- Occupied: 44
- Available: 8

Useful during reallocation.

### Missed Bus Recovery

If student misses Bus 21A, application asks: "Did you miss your bus?"

AI searches:
- Nearby buses
- Available seats
- Similar routes

Suggests: "Take Bus 21B — ETA 8 Minutes — Seats 14"

### AI Travel Assistant

Student asks: "Which bus should I take?"

AI replies: "Take Bus 12, get down at Thiruvanmiyur, walk 250 meters. Estimated Travel: 18 Minutes"

### Afternoon Smart Route Recommendation

- Morning: 10 buses
- Afternoon: Only 5 buses

Student asks: "Which bus goes near my home?"

AI recommends:
- Best bus
- Walking distance
- ETA
- Alternate route

Like Google Maps.

### Emergency SOS

Student presses SOS.

Admin receives:
- Student
- Bus
- GPS
- Time

### Report Problems

Student reports:
- Driver behavior
- Bus cleanliness
- Seat damage
- Overcrowding
- Bus skipped stop

## 3. Driver Module

### Driver Login

Driver logs in securely. Assigned bus automatically appears.

### Start Trip

### End Trip

### Live GPS

- Longitude
- Speed

### Upcoming Stop Reminder

## Project Vision

AGNI Smart Fleet AI is envisioned as a comprehensive AI-powered Campus Transportation Management Platform, not just a live GPS tracker. The goal is to improve safety, operational efficiency, fleet maintenance, and the daily travel experience for students and staff through intelligent automation, real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and role-specific AI assistants. It aims to provide a modern, scalable solution comparable to enterprise fleet management systems while being tailored specifically for the needs of educational institutions.
